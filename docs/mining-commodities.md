## Notes

### Tiers
- **S** - Most valuable, highest difficulty
- **1** - High value minerals
- **2** - Mid-tier minerals
- **3** - Common minerals, easiest to mine
- **U** - Unwanted/inert materials

### Resistance Values
- **Positive resistance** (e.g., 78%) - Harder to break, requires more laser power
- **Negative resistance** (e.g., -33%) - Easier to break, reduces power needed

### Density
Affects how much SCU you get per rock volume:
- **Low** - Less material per rock
- **Medium** - Average yield
- **High** - More material per rock

### Instability
How volatile the rock is during mining:
- **Low** - Easy to control
- **Medium** - Moderate attention needed
- **High** - Requires careful throttle management
- **Very High** - Highly volatile, risk of explosion
